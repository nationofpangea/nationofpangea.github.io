Admissions:



Each year, approximately 100 extremely gifted students are selected to attend the Order of Pangea through a vigorous application process. To be considered for admission, students must demonstrate exceptional intelligence and passion for learning about our world and working to improve it. Pangea does not look at traditional test scores such as ACT or SAT, but instead focuses on finding the brightest young minds of our world who share our deep values and passion for intellectual pursuits through an exclusive and vigorous interview process. Proffesional recommendations are also highly influential in our decision to admit a student. Students interested in applying to the Order of Pangea must fill out a preliminary online application to share their experience, ambitions, and areas of interest. If we deem the student may be a good fit for our school, we will reach out to them to begin the interview process. Only 2.6% of students who begin the interview process ultimately end up accepted at Pangea.



Financial Aid:



The Order of Pangea believes that all students, regardless of financial background, deserve equal opportunity to challenge themselves academically. That is why tuition at Pangea is completely free. Also, room and board in the ship´s luxury living quarters, travel expenses to and from the school for holidays, and any other expenses required for a student´s life while attending our school, are covered by the government of the Sovereign Nation of Pangea.
