Overview:



The Order of Pangea is an innovative and highly selective school for the brilliantly gifted. It is located in and makes up the entirety of the Sovereign Nation of Pangea, established as a sovereign principally in 2019. The Sovereign Nation of Pangea exists on a six-level top-of-the-line cruise ship and the 12 nautical mile radius of sovereign waters that surrounds it. The ship travels through international waters around the world and makes frequent stops at the borders of other countries sovereign waters so that students at the Order of Pangea can visit the mainland countries.
